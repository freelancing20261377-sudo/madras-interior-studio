About Us

Where Vision Meets Craftsmanship

Based in Nedunkundram, Chennai, Madras Interior Studio is a women-owned design firm that has been transforming homes and workspaces into extraordinary experiences. We believe that great interiors are not just about aesthetics—they are about how a space makes you feel.

Our passionate team of designers and craftsmen collaborate closely with every client to deliver bespoke solutions that reflect individual personality and lifestyle. From concept to completion, we curate every detail with precision, care, and budget-conscious planning.
